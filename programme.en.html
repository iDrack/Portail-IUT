Computer Science DUT Program

How is it organised ?

  • The DUT take places in 2 years and those 2 years are divided in 4semesters
  • There is 30 hours of works per week
  • There is 4 group projects during those 4 semesters
  • An end of study internship lasting 10 weeks
  • Possibility to carry on your studies or go to work after your DUT
